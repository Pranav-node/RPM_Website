Our Story

Built for the
Discerning Driver

RPM Motor Works was founded with a single mission — to bring dealership-quality expertise to Pune's luxury car owners without the dealership price tag.

Our team of certified technicians specialises in German engineering — Audi, Mercedes-Benz, Porsche, BMW and Volkswagen. We combine the finest diagnostic equipment with genuine and OEM parts, ensuring your car performs at its absolute best. Located in Katraj, Pune, we're open Monday to Saturday, 10 AM–7 PM.

What We Do

Comprehensive
Workshop Services

From routine maintenance to complex repairs — every service performed with precision, care and expertise.

✨
Detailing & Valeting

Deep interior and exterior detailing using premium products. Your car leaves looking showroom-fresh every single time.

Engine Servicing

Complete engine diagnostics and servicing using genuine or OEM parts. Oil changes, filter replacements, software coding and more.

🏎
Suspension & Steering

Restoration of ride quality and handling precision. Bushings, shock absorbers, tie rods, ball joints — we cover it all.

AC Service & Repair

Complete air conditioning diagnosis, gas recharge and repair. Keep your cabin perfectly conditioned in every season.

Wheel Alignment & Balancing

Precision alignment using laser equipment ensuring even tyre wear, straight tracking and optimal fuel efficiency.

🎨
Denting & Painting

Panel beating and OEM-matched spray painting. We restore your body panels to factory perfection — invisible repairs guaranteed.

🔌
Electrical & Diagnostics

Advanced ECU diagnostics, fault-code reading and electrical system repairs specific to luxury and German car platforms.
